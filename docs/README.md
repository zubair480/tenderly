## The one-line operating principle

**Ship the smallest credible end-to-end experience that makes a judge feel a volunteer can act with confidence today.**

## Canonical external brief

The [AI for Social Good: Hack with MLH & DigitalOcean brief](https://ai-for-social-good-mlh.devpost.com/) is the authority for eligibility, deadline, prizes, and judging. As checked on July 11, 2026, it states a 5:00pm PDT deadline, requires a Devpost submission and in-person judging, and includes tracks for Best Use of DigitalOcean AI Platform, Best Use of Data, and Best UI/UX.
